## About

Hi!I am Asra👋🏼.

Pronouns: <mark>she/her</mark>

I am a third year PhD student at IIIT-Delhi in the Department of Human-Centered Design. I am advised by Prof.<a href="https://pushpendrasingh.me/">Pushpendra Singh</a> as a part of <a href="https://melangeresearch.com/">Mélange Lab. 

My research interest lies in understanding how people interact with information technologies in areas with protracted disruptions due to socio-political conflicts. Since disruptive events are not new, while some are short-lived, some can be a routine part of everyday life. Hence, my primary research interest, particularly in the context of Kashmir, India, is driven by the question of understanding how people who experience a prolonged disruption in their lives use technologies and various Information and Communication Technologies (ICTs) in carrying out their day to day activities. For example, to this effect, I conducted a study investigating the impact of disruption on the educational activities of various stakeholders associated with the educational ecosystem and explored the usage of technologies during disruption to education. I use ethnographic and qualitative methods in my research to study the marginalized populations in conflict-affected regions of Kashmir; more broadly, I am interested in exploring how individuals, groups, communities, organizations, and infrastructures make use of technologies and ICTs during the disruptions to develop resilience. How infrastructural repairs are made during disruptions to carry out everyday activities. My work aims to provide recommendations and design opportunities for systems to address the challenges for these communities.
